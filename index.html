Vingroup
Company Profile

I. Company Overview and Identity

Company Name

Vingroup Joint Stock Company (Vingroup JSC)

Logo

Recognized red-and-white “V” symbol

Year Established

Founded in 1993 in Ukraine as Technocom Corporation; entered Vietnam in the early 2000s

Legal Structure

Joint Stock Company, listed on HOSE under ticker VIC

Mission

To pioneer and lead consumer trends in Vietnam, offering international-standard products and services that improve quality of life

Vision

To become a world-class multi-sector corporation, driving innovation and sustainable development across industries

Core Values

CREDIBILITY- INTEGRITY - CREATIVITY-SPEED - QUALITY - COMPASSION

II. Products and Services

Vingroup operates across six core pillars Tập đoàn Vingroup

01. Industrials & Technology

  • VinFast (automobiles, EVs), VinSmart (smart devices)

02. Real Estate & Services

  • Vinhomes (residential projects), Vincom (shopping malls)

03. Infrastructure

  • Smart city development, housing projects

04. Green Energy

  • Renewable energy initiatives

05. Culture

  • Entertainment, tourism, and lifestyle services

06. Social Enterprises

  • Education (VinSchool, VinUni), healthcare (VinMec hospitals)

Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

  • Vingroup integrates diverse industries under one brand, offering Vietnamese consumers a modern lifestyle with international-standard quality. Its automotive arm, VinFast, is Vietnam’s first global EV brand, symbolizing innovation and national ambition.

    2000s

    Expanded to Vietnam with Vincom (real estate) and Vinpearl (hospitality)

    Launch of VinFast, Vietnam’s first automobile manufacturer

    2020s

    Expansion into global EV markets, education, and healthcare
